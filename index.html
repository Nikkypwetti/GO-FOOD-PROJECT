WHAT IS GO-TAXI

GoTaxi is an application on mobile devices (mobile phones / tablets) that facilitate the Amsterdam people to mobilize in the city area Amsterdam and Surroundings. GoTaxi is available for all Android device users. Equipped with several features that can be used by the Amsterdam community, among others, features Go-Motor (Motor), Go-Car (Taxi Online), GoFood (Food purchasing service), Go-Mart (Service purchase / shopping), GoMassage (Massage Services to your home), GoBox (Car Service Boxes for your business), GoSend (Service Delivery of goods / documents with motor) and GoService (Service such as washing AC). GoTaxi has been present in the city of Amsterdam to help you.
